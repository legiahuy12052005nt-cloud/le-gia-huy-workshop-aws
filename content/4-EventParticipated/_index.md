### [Event 1](4.1-Event1/)
&emsp;**Event Name:** Workshop: AWS Architecture, DevOps Culture & Career Journey

&emsp;**Time:** 09:00 AM, June 13, 2026

&emsp;**Location:** Số 2 đường Hải Triều , phường Bến nghé , Quận 1 , Tp.HCM , Tầng 26

&emsp;**Role in the event:** Attendee

&emsp;**Brief Content Description:** The event offered practical, "battle-tested" insights from industry experts, focusing on four main topics: an authentic look at the working culture in multinational corporations; the reality of a DevOps Engineer's daily work (from incident handling and CI/CD management to Cloud cost optimization); an architectural breakdown of a high-traffic system (specifically a scalable URL Shortener on AWS); and a clear 8-step roadmap for growing from a student into an AWS Partner.

&emsp;**Key Takeaways:** Following the event, I gained a clearer understanding of how real-world systems operate on AWS. This shifted my mindset from merely "writing code" to focusing on "system administration and optimization." I also developed a scalable architecture mindset that I can directly apply to my current internship projects. Most importantly, I learned how to build and polish a professional portfolio to prepare for my upcoming job application journey.
